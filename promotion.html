Frequently Asked Questions

To claim the welcome bonus, simply register an account, make a qualifying deposit of at least $20, and the bonus will be automatically credited to your account. For some promotions, you may need to enter a bonus code during the deposit process or contact customer support.

Wagering requirements refer to the number of times you need to bet the bonus amount before you can withdraw any winnings associated with it. For example, if you receive a $100 bonus with a 35x wagering requirement, you'll need to place bets totaling $3,500 before you can withdraw your winnings.

In most cases, you can only have one active bonus at a time. You need to complete the wagering requirements of your current bonus before claiming another one. However, some promotions like the VIP program benefits can be enjoyed alongside other bonuses. Always check the specific terms and conditions of each promotion.

Our VIP program is based on your activity and loyalty. As you play and wager on our platform, you'll earn loyalty points that determine your VIP level. Silver status is typically achieved within a few weeks of regular play. For higher levels, our VIP team may also personally invite players who show consistent activity and higher deposits.

If you request a withdrawal before meeting the wagering requirements, any remaining bonus funds and associated winnings will be forfeited. You can always check your bonus status and remaining wagering requirements in the "My Bonuses" section of your account.
